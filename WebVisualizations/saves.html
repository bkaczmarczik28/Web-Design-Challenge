Who Needs Constant Saving?

Last Update: 11/16/2019

In this visual, I wanted to display who in our group of intrepid adventurers has made the most saving throws. Typically, I associate saving throws with characters who get into the most trouble or put themselves in the forefront of all the action. I was surprised by the results in the graph above. Caleb tends to stay out of the fray, so I didn't expect him to have made the most saving throws.

I debated on including Molly to this visual, because he is no longer with the party (RIP Molly! We miss you!), but in the end didn't want to exclude him. He has the lowest number of saves, because he was been with the party for the least amount of time, so it stands to reason he would have made the fewest number of saving throws.

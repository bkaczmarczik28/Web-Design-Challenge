How Do You Want To Do This?

Last Update: 11/16/2019

"How Do You Want To Do This" is synonymous now with Critical Role fans everywhere for how do you, the player, want the final killing blow to go? Matt Mercer, the Dungeon Master, always asks this question when a player has successfully rolled enough hit points to finish off the final baddy in a difficult encounter.

As it stands since episode 83, Fjord has made the most killing blows, but not be much, which is encouraging to see. To me, it implies the team is fairly balanced, where everyone gets a punch in. Caduceus having the fewest makes sense, since he spends the most time out of any character healing the party and strengthening their attacks and defense. However, I am surprised Caleb has the same number of killing blows as Caduceus, since he is capable of some powerful attack magic. He even has fewer killing blows then Molly, who has not been with the part for quite some time. I would have expected Caleb and Caduceus to outpace Molly and Yasha for killing blows by now, but maybe it's only a matter of time.

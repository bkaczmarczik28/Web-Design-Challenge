Who is the Talker of the Group?

Last Update: 11/16/2019

For this graph, I focused on depicting how often the characters try to influence others around them with their words rather than through intimidation. I broke up rolls for persuasion and deception to see if one character prefers to lie or be truthful in their approach to swaying another character. I was surprised to see the group is evenly split, where half tend to be more deceitful (Jester, Fjord, and Nott) while the other half tend to be more truthful (Beau, Caleb, Caduceus). Overall, neither roll stands out as significantly more prevalent then another for each character.

As for the biggest talker, Jester takes the win!

And probably the least surprising takeaway from this graph is how little Yasha has rolled for persuasion or deception. Her character is very quiet compared to the others and tends to influence others through action rather than with words. I imagination she has done the most intimidation rolls out of the group.

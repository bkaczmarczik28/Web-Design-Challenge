Natural Rolls - 20s vs 1s

Last Update: 11/16/2019

The graph above displays the percentage rolls where a character rolled a natural 20 or 1 relative to their total number of rolls. I normalized the data by the total number of rolls, because each character operates in a different class, which provides advantages to extra attack rolls. For example, when I originally plotted the straight number of times a natural 20 or 1 was rolled, Beau always came out far ahead, because her character had a significantly larger number of total rolls as compared to any other character. With Beau's class being a monk, she was provided way more opportunities to roll for an attack than any other character. Normalizing the number of times a natural 20 or 1 occurred by total rolls helped to eliminate these class discrepancies between characters.

Depending on your outlook in life, Fjord appears to be the luckiest adventurer with having the highest occurrence of natural 20s, while the Wildmother seems to be keeping an eye out for Caduceus by limiting his number of natural 1s significantly.
